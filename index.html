Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension. Information We Collect The Custom AI Translator extension does not collect or store any personal or identifiable data. However, the extension uses the following permissions: ActiveTab: To access the content on your current browser tab for translation purposes. Storage: To locally save your preferences and settings within your browser. Scripting: To inject scripts required for the functionality of the extension into web pages. ContextMenus: To provide translation options through right-click context menus. Notifications: To notify users about translation results or errors. ClipboardWrite: To allow copying translated text directly to your clipboard. Host permissions: The extension communicates with the Google AI (Gemini API) to perform translations. How We Use Your Information We use the information accessed through permissions solely to provide translation services and functionality directly within your browser. Translated text and user preferences are processed locally or sent directly to Google's Gemini API services. We do not retain or store your translation queries or any translated texts. Data Sharing and Disclosure We do not share, sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. The extension interacts directly with Google's Gemini API, and you should review Google's privacy policy here to understand how Google manages your data. Data Security All data processed by the extension remains within your browser or is securely sent to Google Gemini API services. We implement standard security practices to protect the information during transmission.
